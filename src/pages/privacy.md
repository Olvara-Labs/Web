Olvara Labs is building an on-premises, air-gap capable platform. In such deployments, customer vault data, credentials, secrets, and sensitive operational data are intended to remain under the customer’s control within the customer’s own environment.

Unless a separate written agreement states otherwise, Olvara Labs does not require access to customer vault contents for the public website or ordinary business enquiries.

If support, diagnostics, pilot evaluation, or design partner collaboration requires sharing logs or technical information, the scope, method, security controls, and confidentiality obligations should be defined in a separate written agreement.
